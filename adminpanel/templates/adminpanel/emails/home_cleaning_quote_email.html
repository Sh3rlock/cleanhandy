Our TheCleanHandy Cleaning Service Checklist

Bedrooms & Living Spaces

Flat Rate Bedroom Checklist

  • Tidy up beds and replace linens if provided
  • Dust and clean all reachable surfaces
  • Sanitize handles, switches, and high-touch areas
  • Clean mirrors and glass accents
  • Swiffer or mop the floors
  • Empty trash and recycling bins

Bathrooms

Flat Rate Bathroom Cleaning Checklist

  • Disinfect and clean toilet, shower, and sink areas
  • Dust and wipe down all accessible surfaces
  • Sanitize doorknobs and light switches
  • Polish mirrors and any glass elements
  • Sweep and mop the floors with Swiffer
  • Remove garbage and recyclables

Kitchen

Flat Rate Kitchen Cleaning Checklist

  • Clean and scrub the sink thoroughly
  • Wipe down cabinet fronts and appliance exteriors
  • Sanitize inside and outside of the microwave
  • Clean and disinfect all countertop surfaces
  • Swiffer floors to remove dirt and debris
  • Dust and wipe down all accessible surfaces
  • Sanitize light switches and door handles
  • Polish glass and mirror surfaces
  • Take out trash and recycling
