Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2025

CleanHandy LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit https://www.cleanhandy.com or use any related mobile application, tools, or services (collectively, the "Platform").

By accessing or using the Platform, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use the Platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

  1. Provide & operate the Platform – create accounts, process bookings, facilitate payments, send confirmations and reminders.
  2. Verify Pros – conduct background checks and validate insurance or license information.
  3. Communicate with you – respond to inquiries, send transactional notices, and deliver service or policy updates.
  4. Personalize user experience – understand preferences, recommend services, and display tailored content or ads.
  5. Process payments – via our payment processor, Stripe (card data is transmitted directly to Stripe and never stored on our servers).
  6. Ensure safety & security – detect fraud, abuse, or security incidents and protect against harm.
  7. Comply with legal obligations – maintain records for tax, accounting, and regulatory reporting.
  8. Improve the Platform – perform analytics, debug errors, and develop new features.
  9. Market our services – send promotional emails or push notifications (you may opt out at any time).

3. Legal Bases for Processing (U.S.)

Although U.S. privacy laws do not generally require a legal basis, we process personal information under the following common‑sense grounds:

  • Contractual necessity – to perform the services you request;
  • Legitimate interests – to operate and improve our business;
  • Consent – for optional marketing communications or precise location collection;
  • Legal obligations – to comply with tax, accounting, and other laws.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards (e.g., TLS encryption, access controls, periodic penetration testing) to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Typical retention periods are:

  • Account data: for as long as the account is active and up to 7 years after closure;
  • Booking and payment records: 7 years (IRS record‑keeping);
  • Customer‑support communications: 2 years.

9. International Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. If you access the Platform from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S., where data‑protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

10. Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

The Platform is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact privacy@cleanhandy.com and we will delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy periodically. We will post the revised Policy on the Platform and, if the changes are material, provide at least 14 days’ advance notice via email or in‑app notification before the new Policy takes effect.
